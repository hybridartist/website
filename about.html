About

Hi! I'm Shannon Holloway, a designer and researcher based in Brooklyn, NY.

I'm passionate about solving complex problems with human-centered design methods. I enjoy each phase of the process - discovering unmet user needs, synthesizing research findings into actionable insights, and designing innovative and feasible design solutions fit for human use.

I received my BA from the University of California at Berkeley and am currently pursuing an MS at New York University Tandon School of Engineering in Integrated Digital Media. I'm a James Dyson Design Leader and member of the Human Factors and Ergonomics Society. the Environmental Design Research Association. and Design for America NYU.

In my free time I enjoy working on my own artistic and DIY projects, riding my bike or getting lost in a good sci-fi novel.
